Quality Life Insurance Starts Here

Tuesday, February 9 2019 Jonathan

In recent months a wave of speculation has broken over the life insurance industry, and with good reason. Lax standards have left too many Australians with policies that might not be paid out. For Aussies simply wanting to protect their family there’s anger, confusion and growing mistrust.

At Life Insurance Comparison, we ONLY compare quality life insurance products – that’s why all of our policies are fully underwritten at the time of purchase. What that means is that your risk factors, like your medical condition and profession, are assessed before you lock in your policy. The benefit of doing this is that it leaves you certain of exactly what you’re covered for. As a result, when you make a claim you can do so with confidence. It’s not a dodgy, quick fix, it’s an affordable, secure solution.

This is just one aspect of the comprehensive, free service provided by our Sydney-based, professional advisers. From finding the cheapest provider for your situation from our panel of leading insurers, to negotiating lower premiums on your behalf and fighting for customers who have previously been denied insurance – our aim is to make quality life insurance an easy, affordable reality for all Australians.

Case Study: Maggie Forster
After years of paying premiums to the same insurer, Maggie found herself in trouble when they suddenly refused to renew her policy. Due to a change in her health and her finances, she was told that no insurers would protect her. That’s when she came to us.

Our advisers were not only able to get Maggie the peace of mind that an underwritten life insurance policy brings; they also helped her save a few thousand dollars on her yearly premium. She was even able to save on her out of pocket expenses by opting to fund the new premium through her SMSF (self-managed super fund).

Maggie’s story showcases the value we’re able to offer our customers. The size of our company and the close relationships we maintain with our partners means that we have negotiating power. In Maggie’s case this allowed us to secure her a better underwriting outcome than she was offered when she approached the insurer directly.

At Life Insurance Comparison we work with both you and the insurers. Our goal is to bridge the gap between people who struggle to understand the complexity of the life insurance industry and quality, affordable products that offer real protection.

Our clients trust us because we work hard to secure the best outcome for them, and we provide a comprehensive service that even includes ongoing support for the claims process and yearly reviews.

Having quality life insurance means you know exactly what you’re covered for, and most importantly, that you feel secure in your cover. Life insurance is about protecting against risk, so why take a chance on the insurance you’re buying? Get quality life insurance today.
