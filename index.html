Income protection, TPD, and Trauma insurance

Besides life insurance, there are other insurances or add ons that can help secure your family’s financial future and get peace of mind knowing there is a plan when you are ill or injured.

Income protection Insurance

Income protection insurance, also known as salary protection insurance can provide monthly benefits if you are unable to work due to any injury, illness or involuntary unemployment.

TPD Insurance

Total and permanent disability insurance is an optional add on to your life insurance that will provide you with a lump sum benefit if you suffer an injury or illness that leaves you totally and permanently disabled.

Trauma Insurance

Trauma insurance will allow you to claim a lump sum payment If you are diagnosed with a critical illness such as cancer or stroke that will make a significant impact on your life.
